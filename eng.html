ABOUT
US

Karagiannis Brazil was established in 2016 as a subsidiary company of Karagiannis Group. It is offering complete solutions in terms of constructing and operating photovoltaic projects in Brazil. Our parent company was founded in 1954 and has been dealing with all forms of electromechanical and civil installations. The first important project was the electrical installation of Drama’s General Hospital in 1955 and since then many have followed and have always been delivered on time and with quality being our main priority. Our disposal dedicated members fully accomplish a quick and correct management of each project. Specifically, we have 5 electrical engineers, 10 junior engineers, 15 experienced site managers and 250 technicians and workers that can always be increased if the project demands, along with 20 administrative staﬀ members In addition, we provide our personnel with the best equipment for the construction, installation and testing of our work.
